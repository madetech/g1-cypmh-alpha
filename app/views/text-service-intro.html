If you'd like to text someone about how you're feeling now, rather than find help, these services are available:

  • tic+chat is an anonymous, safe, confidential, 1-2-1 support service for young people aged 9-21 living in Gloucestershire. It's open 5-9pm, Sunday to Thursday.
  • Kooth provides free online counselling and a support network, available 24/7.
  • You can call Childline on 0800 1111 from 7.30am to 3.30am Monday to Friday, and 9am to 3.30am Saturday and Sunday.
